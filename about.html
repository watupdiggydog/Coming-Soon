About Diggy Dogs Wash

Zoe Rae


Manager


My name is Zoe-Rae, I am a mother of 3 human babies and 3 Fur-babies. My passion for animals started at a very young age. From the moment I could walk and saw a dog followed It home. I spent all my free time with animals. Going on Adventures with my dog Axel and horse riding every chance I got. As soon as I was old enough I began doing volunteer work at the RSPCA and AWQL. My top priority is making sure you Doggo is happy and safe..
