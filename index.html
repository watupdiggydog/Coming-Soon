About Our Stylists

Zoe-Rae


Manager


My name is Zoe-Rae, a proud mother of three human babies, three fur-babies, and I happily manage and operate Diggy Dogs Wash. My passion for all animals began from a young age. The moment I first saw a dog, I followed it home, I was two, and I knew I needed this pup in my life. As I grew up, I spent all my free time with animals, adventuring with Axel, my Shepherd cross Kelpie, and every chance I could, I was horse riding. As soon as I was old enough, I began volunteering with the RSPCA and AWQL. Please know my top priority is to ensure your doggo is safe and happy.

Bath and tidy

Hydro-bath in a shampoo, followed by Conditioner. Your pup will then be dried using the dryer. Your fur baby will then have hair around the eyes trimmed. Paw pads shaved and sanitary areas shaved. A bath and blow dry can not be done if the dog is matted.

Puppy Groom

Hydro-bath in a shampoo product suited to your pups skin and coat type, followed by Conditioner. Your pup will then be dried using the dryer. Your fur baby will then have hair around the eyes trimmed. Paw tidied and sanitary areas shaved. A puppy groom can not be done if there is matting present.

Book now

Full Groom

Hydro-bath in a shampoo, followed by Conditioner. Your pup will then be fully dried using the dryer. Then pup will have a full body clip with either fluffy face and tail or short all over.



Book now

Deshed

Hydro-bath in our deshedding shampoo, followed by Conditioner. Your pup will then be dried using the dryer which helps release the undercoat. Pup will also receive a good brush out to remove any knots and help remove more undercoat.
